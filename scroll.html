Address Bar Spoof Simulation via Scroll Behavior

By: Test Researcher | Published: September 17, 2025

This demonstration shows how scrolling down a webpage can hide the browser’s address bar. Attackers could exploit this to spoof the URL and trick users into believing they are interacting with a legitimate website.

The hidden address bar allows malicious interfaces to simulate the browser’s UI, potentially leading to phishing attacks or unauthorized data collection.

The issue was responsibly disclosed in this test environment to highlight risks associated with dynamic UI changes during scrolling interactions.
